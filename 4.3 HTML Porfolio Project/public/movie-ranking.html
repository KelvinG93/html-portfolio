The Best Movies According to Kelvin.

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Blood and Bone

An ex-con takes the underfround fighting world by storm in his quest to fulfill a promise to a dead friend.

2. Kill Bill: Vol.1

After waking from a 4-year coma, a former assassin wreaks vengeance on the team of assassins who betrayed her.

3. Alien

After investigating a mysterious transmission of unknown origin, the crew of a commercial spacecraft encounters a deadly lifeform.
